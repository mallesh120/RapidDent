Privacy Policy for RapidDent

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

Introduction

RapidDent ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

RapidDent is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics through Firebase Analytics to improve app performance and user experience.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for analytics purposes.
  • Progress Data: Your exam progress, scores, and study session data are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our app functionality
  • To improve and optimize the user experience
  • To analyze app usage patterns and trends
  • To fix bugs and improve app performance

Data Storage and Security

Your study progress and personal data are stored locally on your device. We use Firebase services for analytics and app functionality, which may process data according to Google's privacy policies. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For analytics, authentication, and cloud storage. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with third parties for analytics purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Data Retention

We retain analytics data for as long as necessary to provide our services and improve the app. You can delete your local app data at any time by uninstalling the application.
